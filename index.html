Dish Description: A sweet and aromatic yellow rice dessert flavored with saffron, cardamom, and rosewater, topped with raisins and nuts. Often served on festive occasions.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 tsp saffron threads
  • 1 tbsp rosewater
  • 1/2 tsp ground cardamom
  • 2 tbsp butter or ghee
  • 2 tbsp raisins

  2. Boil until 70% cooked, then drain again.
  3. Melt butter, add rice, saffron, sugar, rosewater, cardamom, and coloring. Steam covered.
  4. Top with raisins and nuts before serving.
